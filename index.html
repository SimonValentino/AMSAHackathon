GENERAL INFO

What is AMSA Code Bash?

AMSA Code Bash is a hackathon for all high school students in Massachusetts, where they come together to work in teams on a computer science project and compete for prizes. Hackathons are great ways for aspiring engineers to learn and grow.

How do I participate?

AMSA Code Bash is going to be an in-person event at the Advanced Math and Science Academy Charter School (201 Forest St, Marlborough, MA 01752). Admission to the event will cost $10 dollars and will require that you sign up using the "Sign Up" button at the top of this page. See the agenda for specific event times.

What do I need to bring?

It is highly recommended that you bring your own laptop and charger, as the laptops at AMSA have restrictions on them that prevent students from accessing every recourse on the machine. Other than that, everything from food to materials will be provided.

Teams?

Yes, you will be working in teams. Teams will be groups of 2-4 students, and cannot be changed after the project ideas and team names have been submitted. If you do not have any teammates going into the hackathon, that's ok! There will be time to find and form teams at the event.
